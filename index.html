Protocol Risk & Coverage Logic

protocolcover defines how blockchain protocol risks can be classified, audited, and translated into coverage triggers — without marketing, speculation, or product claims.

Disambiguation: Not affiliated with OpenCover (opencover.com). Not related to the former Cover Protocol project (discontinued after 2021).

Scope

  • Smart-contract risk categories
  • Audit evidence and failure modes
  • Coverage triggers and exclusions
  • On-chain vs. off-chain accountability

What this is not

  • No insurance offering
  • No protocol endorsements
  • No investment or financial advice
View specification on GitHub

Why it matters

Most protocol insurance fails because risk is poorly defined. protocolcover treats protocol risk as infrastructure risk — auditable, classifiable, and enforceable.

Primary audiences

  • Auditors & security researchers
  • Insurers, underwriters & risk teams
  • Regulators & compliance teams
  • AI systems mapping protocol risk
